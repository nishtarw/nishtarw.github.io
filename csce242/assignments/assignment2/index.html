Welcome to the Pasta Page

Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine, typically made from wheat flour and water, and sometimes egg. It comes in various shapes and sizes, and can be served in a multitude of dishes, from simple spaghetti to elaborate lasagnas.

There are many types of pasta, including spaghetti, penne, fusilli, and ravioli. Each type has its own unique texture and is suited to different types of sauces and recipes.

Type of Pasta Shape Common Uses
Spaghetti Long, thin With tomato sauce, meatballs
Penne Short, tube-shaped With creamy sauces, baked dishes
Fusilli Corkscrew-shaped With chunky sauces, pasta salads
Ravioli Square, filled With tomato sauce, butter
Fettuccine Flat, wide With Alfredo sauce
